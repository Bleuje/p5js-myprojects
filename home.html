Vector field drawing

After I found out about Processing, I found rapidly Daniel Shiffman's excellent videos. More precisely I find p5.js great because it makes it so easy to share your work. This vector drawing tool was a nice project to discover lots of features from Processing/p5.js.

About the project content : some particles paint a canvas, guided by forces generated from Perlin Noise. The physics and colors are very customizable.
